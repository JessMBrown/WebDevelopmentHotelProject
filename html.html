THE SPA


EXPLORE OUR TREATMENTS


Step into a world of tranquility and rejuvenation with our exquisite array of spa treatments.


Sea Salt Body Massage

Indulge in the rejuvenating sensation of our Sea Salt Body Massage, a luxurious spa treatment designed to renew both body and mind.

Harnessing the therapeutic properties of natural sea salt, this exfoliating massage gently sloughs away dead skin cells, leaving your skin silky smooth and deeply moisturized.


What's Included
  • Relaxing long strokes
  • Gentle kneading and circular motions
  • Targeted friction techniques
  • Passive joint movement

Total treatment duration: Typically 60-90 minutes

Deep Tissue Massage

Experience the ultimate relief with our Deep Tissue Body Massage, a targeted therapy designed to release tension and restore balance.

Our skilled therapists use firm pressure and slow strokes to penetrate deep into muscles, alleviating chronic pain and promoting relaxation.



What's Included
  • Intense pressure targeting deep muscle layers
  • Firm kneading and friction technique
  • Focus on releasing chronic tension and knots
  • Focus on specific areas of discomfort or injury

Total treatment duration: Typically 60-90 minutes

Hot Stone Therapy

Indulge in the soothing warmth of our Hot Stone Therapy, a blissful treatment that harmonizes body and soul.

Smooth, heated stones are strategically placed on key points of your body to melt away tension and promote deep relaxation.



What's Included
  • Placement of smooth, heated stones on key points of the body
  • Application of gentle pressure to release tension and promote relaxation
  • Warm stone massage techniques to enhance circulation and soothe muscles
  • Smooth, gliding strokes using the stones

Total treatment duration: Typically 60-90 minutes

Body Scrub - Exfoliation

Indulge your skin in the ultimate pampering experience with our luxurious Body Scrub Treatment. Let the worries of the day melt away as our expert therapists gently exfoliate your skin, removing dead cells and revealing a radiant glow beneath.

Treat yourself to the rejuvenating benefits of our Body Scrub Treatment and unveil skin that feels refreshed, revitalized, and utterly pampered.


What's Included
  • Exfoliation with natural scrub ingredients
  • Relaxing massage to apply the scrub
  • Cleansing and rejuvenation of the skin
  • Hydration with moisturizing products

The Facial

Indulge your skin in a luxurious journey of rejuvenation with our Facial Treatment. Tailored to meet your specific skincare needs, this pampering experience begins with a thorough analysis by our expert aestheticians.

What's Included
