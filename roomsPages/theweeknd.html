Designed by The Weeknd ‘Electronic dance fusion’ Abel Makkonen Tesfaye, born February 16, 1990, known professionally as the Weeknd, is a Canadian singer and songwriter. He is known for his unconventional music production, artistic reinventions, and signature use of the falsetto register. Top hits include Starbou, Blinding Lights and Die for You.
