Designed by Panic at the disco ‘A boroque meets Rock and Pop’ Panic! at the Disco is an American pop rock band from Las Vegas, Nevada, formed in 2004 by childhood friends Ryan Ross, Spencer Smith, Brent Wilson, and Brendon Urie. Following several lineup changes, Panic! at the Disco operated as the solo project of frontman Urie from 2015 until its discontinuation in 2023. Top hits include high hopes, house of memories and I write sins not tragedies. www.panicatthedisco.com
