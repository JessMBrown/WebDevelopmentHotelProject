Designed by Twenty One Pilots ‘Indietronica meets Hiphop’ Twenty One Pilots are an American musical duo from Columbus, Ohio. Initially a band, the group was formed in 2009 by lead vocalist Tyler Joseph along with Nick Thomas and Chris Salih, who both left in 2011. Since their departure, the line-up has consisted of Joseph and drummer Josh Dun. Top hits include Stressed Out, Heathens and Ride www.twentyonepilots.com
