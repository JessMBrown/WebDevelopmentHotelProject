Our Performance Room is more than just a venue; it's a hub of creativity, a sanctuary for expression, and a haven for those seeking vibrant entertainment. Whatever you're craving, our lineup of gigs, comedy nights, and dancing events ensures there's something for everyone.

Wednesday Rock night

Wednesday night is rock night! Experience the magic of legendary rock bands recreated right before your eyes! From Led Zeppelin to Queen, Fleetwood Mac to The Rolling Stones, we welcome tribute bands who capture the essence of these icons, delivering unforgettable performances that will have you singing along all night long.

Thursday Night Comedy Club

Prepare to be entertained by top-notch stand-up comedians who will have you in stitches from start to finish! From sharp-witted observations to laugh-out-loud anecdotes, our lineup of comedians promises to deliver unforgettable performances that will leave you grinning ear to ear.

Jam Fridays

Start your weekend right at Jam Fridays! Immerse yourself in the sounds of our local music scene as talented bands and musicians take the stage! From indie rock to soulful ballads, our lineup promises a diverse range of musical genres to satisfy every taste and keep you grooving all night long

Saturday Speakeasy

Step back in time and experience the allure of a bygone era at our weekly Saturday Night Speakeasy! Join us for an evening of cocktails, live jazz music, and a hint of mystery. Slip into your finest attire and indulge in the clandestine ambiance as we transport you to the roaring twenties.

Sunday Social Dance

Get ready to move and groove at our Social Dance Sundays! Join us for a fun-filled evening of dance classes followed by a vibrant dancefloor with live music! Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned dancer, our expert instructors will lead you through exciting dance routines to get you into the rhythm. After the classes, hit the dancefloor and show off your new moves while enjoying electrifying live music performances. From salsa to swing, there's something for everyone to dance the night away.
